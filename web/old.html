APSIPA Taiwan Chapter

The first APSIPA Chapter covering a whole area of Taiwan (APSIPA Japan Chapter) was established in Taiwan in 2020.

Kick-off Event and Seminars (8:00 AM–17:30 PM, December 19, 2020)

The following seminars will be held at 8:00 AM~ 17:30 PM, December 19, 2020 (Taiwan Time GMT+8) at NCKU International conference center.

Physical Conference in Taiwan & Virtual International: Co-Located with ICS 2020 @NCKU's International conference center on Dec 19, 2020.
